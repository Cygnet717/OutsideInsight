Dyslexis Resources

  • Dyslexia questionare - Take this Dyslexic Questionnaire to find out if you or someone you know is dyslexic.
  • The International Dyslexia Association - Promoting literacy through research, education and advocacy.
  • Barton Reading and Spelling - Tutoring for dyslexic individuals who want to read, write and spell with their peers.
  • Bright Solutions for Dyslexia - Sharing the latest dyslexia research with those who need to know.
  • DIBELS - Dynamic Indicators of Basic Early Literacy Skills (free testing available for schools).
  • Learning Ally - Making reading available to Blind and Dyslexic people. School books available in audio format!
  • Pacer Center MN - Helping families with children and young adults with disabilities.
  • Fair Test - National Center for Fair and Open Testing, listing colleges and universities not requiring SAT or ACT scores.
  • Learning Disabilities Association of America - Providing support to people with learning disabilities since 1963
  • Government Disabliity Services - Comprehensive information on disability programs and services in communities nationwide.
  • Project Genesis - Believes that all persons, regardless of their disability, have the right to enjoy the same full range of life experiences which others in their community enjoy.
  • Irlen Lenses - Scotopic Sensitivity - When words move on the page or reading creates headaches or nausea, this could be the reason.
