Screening

Screening for dyslexia is appropriate for children or adults and will cover a battery of individual tests, looking at working memory, auditory memory, processing speeds, auditory discrimination, phonological awareness and memory. For children this will also take into consideration school records such as Individualized Education Plans (IEPs), Accommodations, 504 plans, Report Cards. Screening for dyslexia can give you peace of mind, answers, knowledge, understanding, at any age. Ask for a free consultation to review your IEP goals and accommodations.

Tutoring

Several methods and programs are available. Find a certified tutor who works with an Orton-Gillingham based system. Tutoring will begin before phonics to focus on blending and breaking apart phonemes (the sounds of our language). Phonics will not work until the sounds can be broken apart and pulled together. A good tutoring system will work on reading, spelling and writing and will go at the pace of the individual being tutored.  The Barton Reading and Spelling system is used at Outside Insight, LLC.
